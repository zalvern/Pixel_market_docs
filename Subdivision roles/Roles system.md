# Roles system:
Resources collected by the gathering division will be given to the trading division to be exchanged for gems and BC, that will be redistributed among all staff.
## Gathering division
The gathering division has 5 subdivisions:

Mining: Mines resources.
Farming: Farms resources from seeds.
Fishing: Acquires fish using fishing gear.
Event grinding: High activity during events to acquire limited and very rare / OP gear/items.
## Trading division:
The trading division has 2 subdivisions:

Individual trading: Uses PMPH forum system to arrange individual trades, then give earnings back to high command for distribution.
Mass trading: Use PWE machines to place sale orders.
